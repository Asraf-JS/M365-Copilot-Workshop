M365 Copilot Workshop

Microsoft 365 Copilot Training

Prompts, reference notes, and sample datasets. Each topic has a Notes page and a Prompts page.

Workshop Scenario

You are creating an AI Usage Guide for your department. You will research it in Copilot Chat, draft it in Pages, refine it as a proposal in Word, email it for approval in Outlook, present it in PowerPoint, collect feedback with Forms, analyse results in Excel, and finish with a hands-on Copilot Studio agent.
